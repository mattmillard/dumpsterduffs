## 📄 FAQ PAGE COPY (Full Draft)

### General Questions

**Q: How much does a dumpster rental cost in Missouri?**  
A: Our 15-yard dumpster rental starts at $299 for up to 3 days. This all-inclusive price covers delivery, pickup, disposal (up to 2 tons), and all fees. No fuel charges, no surprise costs. Need to keep it longer? We charge $15/day for extended rentals.

**Q: What size dumpster do I need for my project?**  
A: Our 15-yard dumpster is perfect for most residential and small commercial projects. It holds about 4-5 pickup truck loads and is ideal for:

- Kitchen or bathroom remodels
- Garage, attic, or basement cleanouts
- Roofing jobs (up to 2,000 sq ft)
- Small deck or shed removal
- Landscaping and yard debris
- Estate cleanouts  
  Not sure? Call us at (573) 356-4272 and we'll help you figure it out.

**Q: How long can I keep the dumpster?**  
A: Standard rental is 3 days, but we're flexible. Keep it as long as you need—just let us know. Extended rentals are $15/day. Most customers finish within 3-5 days, but we've had dumpsters out for 3 weeks for larger projects. No pressure.

**Q: How quickly can you deliver?**  
A: Most orders are delivered within 2-4 hours during business hours. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM Monday-Friday. Weekend delivery available by request. We'll give you a delivery window when you book.

**Q: What can I put in the dumpster?**  
A: We accept most household junk, construction debris, furniture, appliances (except refrigerators with Freon), yard waste, shingles, and wood. Basically, if it's solid waste from a home or job site, it's probably fine.

**Q: What CAN'T go in the dumpster?**  
A: For safety and legal reasons, we CANNOT accept:

- Hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, oil, gasoline)
- Tires
- Batteries
- Electronics (computers, TVs)
- Asbestos
- Mattresses (in some areas)  
  If you're unsure about an item, call us before loading.

**Q: Do I need a permit?**  
A: If the dumpster is placed on your private property (driveway, yard), you usually don't need a permit. If it's on the street or public property, you may need a permit from your city. We can guide you through the process. Columbia requires permits for street placement; Jefferson City handles it case-by-case. Call your city hall or ask us for help.

**Q: What's included in the price?**  
A: Everything. Delivery, pickup, disposal (up to 2 tons), and the rental itself. No fuel surcharges, no delivery fees, no hidden costs. The price you see is the price you pay.

**Q: Is there a weight limit?**  
A: Yes, 2 tons (4,000 lbs) is included in the base price. If you go over, we charge $75/ton for the overage. For reference, a kitchen remodel rarely hits 2 tons. Concrete and roofing debris add up fast, so let us know if you're disposing of heavy materials.

**Q: Can I move the dumpster once it's delivered?**  
A: No, once it's placed, it stays put. We have special equipment to lift and move it. If you realize you need it in a different spot, call us and we'll reposition it for free (if we can do it on the same visit).

**Q: What if I finish early?**  
A: Call us and we'll pick it up. We don't prorate early pickups, but we're happy to get it out of your way as soon as you're done.

**Q: What if I need it longer?**

### Pricing & Payment

**Q: When do I pay?**  
A: We collect payment at booking (credit card, debit card, or check). Your price is locked in when you reserve—no surprise charges at pickup.

**Q: Do you charge a deposit?**  
A: Nope. Full payment up front, no deposit required.

**Q: Are there any hidden fees?**  
A: Absolutely not. The price we quote is the price you pay. Delivery, pickup, disposal (up to 2 tons), and the rental itself are all included. The only additional charges would be:

- Extended rental ($15/day)
- Weight overage ($75/ton over 2 tons)
- Loading service ($149, if requested)  
  We'll always tell you upfront if any of these apply.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for contractors?**  
A: Yes! If you're a contractor or need multiple dumpsters per year, ask about our contractor discount program. We offer volume discounts, NET payment terms, and a dedicated rep for repeat customers.
