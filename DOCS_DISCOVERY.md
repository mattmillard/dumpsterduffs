## CRITICAL CONVERSION BLOCKERS 🚨

### 1. Price Opacity (HIGH IMPACT)

- ❌ No pricing visible anywhere on site
- ❌ Forces 100% of prospects to call or email
- ❌ Murders mobile conversion (users expect instant info)
- 📊 **Impact:** Likely losing 60-70% of potential leads who price shop

### 2. Overwhelming Vagueness

- ❌ What's included in rental? (delivery, disposal, pickup?)
- ❌ How long can I keep it?
- ❌ What CAN'T go in it?
- ❌ Do I need a permit?
- 📊 **Impact:** Creates anxiety, increases bounce rate

### 3. Weak Trust Building

- ✓ Veteran badge exists BUT it's tiny
- ❌ No customer reviews visible
- ❌ No "1,200+ jobs completed" type stat
- ❌ No insurance/licensing proof
- ❌ No on-time guarantee or service promise
- 📊 **Impact:** First-time renters need more reassurance

### 4. Call-to-Action Confusion

- ❌ Two competing CTAs: "Book Now" and "Contact Us"
- ❌ "Book Now" button goes to basic calendar (no context)
- ❌ No clear user journey: "Quote → Book → Confirm"
- 📊 **Impact:** Users unsure what action to take first

### 5. Mobile Friction

- ❌ No sticky call button on mobile
- ❌ Form likely too long/generic for mobile completion
- ❌ No "tap to call" optimization
- 📊 **Impact:** Mobile is 60%+ of traffic—critical missed opportunity

### 6. One Size = One Problem

- ⚠️ Only offering 15-yard limits market
- ❌ No explanation of WHY this size works for most projects
- ❌ No size comparison (e.g., "holds 4-5 pickup truck loads")
- ❌ Users unsure if it fits their project
- 📊 **Impact:** Prospects self-disqualify or call competitors with "more options"

## CLARITY GAPS 🤔

### Process Questions (Unanswered)

- How long is standard rental?
- What happens if I need it longer?
- What if I overfill it?
- How much notice for delivery?
- Same-day delivery—always, or depends?
- Can I move it once it's dropped?

### Service Questions (Unanswered)

- Service area boundaries? (Just Columbia? All of 573 area?)
- What's included in price vs. extra fees?
- How do I know if I need a permit?
- What debris types are banned?
- Is there a weight limit?

### Loading Service Questions (Underexplained)

- How much does loading cost?
- What does it include?
- How do I request it?
- Is it available same-day?
- 📊 **Opportunity:** This is a HUGE differentiator but hidden in fine print
